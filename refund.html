Refund Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026. This Refund Policy applies to private tours, luxury transport, airport transfers, chauffeur services, and related reservations arranged by CityZen Tours.

Deposits and Reservations

Some bookings may require a deposit to reserve vehicles, drivers, guides, hotels, camps, or partner services. Deposit terms will be shared before confirmation.

Client Cancellations

Cancellations made more than 7 days before the service date may be eligible for a partial refund after deducting non-refundable partner costs. Cancellations within 7 days may be non-refundable depending on the service and suppliers involved.

Same-Day Transport

Airport transfers, chauffeur service, and same-day transport cancellations may be charged if the driver or vehicle has already been dispatched.

Changes to Bookings

We will do our best to adjust dates, pickup times, routes, and vehicle categories when availability allows. Price changes may apply for revised services.

Weather and Force Majeure

If weather, road closures, safety concerns, or events beyond our control affect your trip, we may offer an alternative route, date change, credit, or partial refund depending on the circumstances.
